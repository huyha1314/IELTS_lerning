# 🗓️ Your First 30 Days: IELTS Action Plan (June 3 - July 2)

This document is your **daily master plan** for the first 30 days of your 6-month journey. It maps out exactly what to do every single day, integrating your books (*Cambridge, Grammar in Use, Oxford Word Skills, Target Band 7*) with the best online resources (*IELTS Liz, IELTS Advantage, Podcasts*).

**Daily Commitment:** 60 - 75 Minutes (5 High-Intensity Days + 1 Flex Day).
**Rule:** If you make a mistake, log it in your **Error Log Notebook** (with a heavy focus on spelling).

---

### 🟢 Week 1: Launch & Baseline (June 3 - June 7)
**Materials:** **[B1] Grammar**, **[B2] Vocab**, **[T9] Test 9**

*   **Day 1: Wednesday, June 3 (Writing Task 1 & Grammar)**
    *   **Task 1 (15m):** Do 1 Unit of **[B1] Grammar** (Focus on Tenses). 
    *   **Task 2 (45m):** Watch an **IELTS Liz** video on Writing Task 1. Write a 150-word practice report describing a line graph about data storage trends.
    *   **KPI:** Write a 150-word report with 0 basic tense mistakes and aggressive proofreading.
*   **Day 2: Thursday, June 4 (Reading & Vocab)**
    *   **Task 1 (15m):** Do 1 Unit of **[B2] Vocab**.
    *   **Task 2 (45m):** Take 1 Reading Passage from **[T9] Test 9**. **No time limit.**
    *   **KPI:** Score at least 9/13 on the passage. Deploy 3 new words in a sentence about your ultra-marathon.
*   **Day 3: Friday, June 5 (Flex Day)**
    *   **Task:** If tired from lab work/training, rest completely. If you have energy, spend 30 minutes writing sentences using new vocabulary.
    *   **KPI:** Recover cognitive load.
*   **Day 4: Saturday, June 6 (Writing Task 2 Structure)**
    *   **Task 1 (15m):** Watch an **IELTS Advantage** video on Task 2 essay structure.
    *   **Task 2 (60m):** Write one full Task 2 essay. Use simple but correct grammar.
    *   **KPI:** Complete a 250-word essay with a clear thesis statement. Zero spelling errors.
*   **Day 5: Sunday, June 7 (Rest & Reevaluate - 15 mins)**
    *   **Task:** Review your Error Log. Are your spelling mistakes decreasing? Rest for the rest of the day.

---

### 🟢 Week 2: Building Core Skills (June 8 - June 14)

*   **Day 6: Monday, June 8 (Reading Strategy)**
    *   **Task 1 (30m):** Read the Reading Strategies section in *Target Band 7*. Focus on Skimming and Scanning.
    *   **Task 2 (75m):** Do 2 Reading Passages from *Cambridge IELTS 9*. Set a timer for 40 minutes.
    *   **KPI:** Finish both passages within 40 minutes. Understand *why* you got True/False/Not Given wrong.
*   **Day 7: Tuesday, June 9 (Listening Strategy)**
    *   **Task 1 (45m):** Listen to 1 episode of **IELTS Speaking for Success** podcast. Write down 5 new academic phrases.
    *   **Task 2 (60m):** Watch an **IELTS Liz** YouTube video on Listening (Map labeling or Multiple Choice). Do Sections 3 & 4 of a Listening Test from *Cambridge IELTS 9*.
    *   **KPI:** Accurately answer map/multiple-choice questions. Score 12/20 on the hard sections.
*   **Day 8: Wednesday, June 10 (Writing Task 1 - Maps/Processes)**
    *   **Task 1 (45m):** Watch an **IELTS Liz** video on Writing Task 1 (Maps or Process Diagrams). 
    *   **Task 2 (60m):** Do 2 Units of *Grammar in Use* (Passive voice - crucial for process diagrams). Write 1 practice Task 1 (Process Diagram).
    *   **KPI:** Use passive voice correctly at least 5 times in your report.
*   **Day 9: Thursday, June 11 (Reading Practice)**
    *   **Task 1 (45m):** Do 1 new Unit of *Oxford Word Skills*. Review last week's words.
    *   **Task 2 (60m):** Take 1 Reading Passage from *Cambridge IELTS 10*. Focus on "Matching Headings" questions.
    *   **KPI:** Get 100% correct on the Matching Headings questions.
*   **Day 10: Friday, June 12 (Writing Task 2 - Idea Generation)**
    *   **Task 1 (30m):** Watch an **IELTS Advantage** video on generating ideas for Task 2.
    *   **Task 2 (75m):** Write one full Task 2 essay (Discuss Both Views topic). Focus on clearly separating the two views into Body Paragraph 1 and 2.
    *   **KPI:** Use at least 3 advanced vocabulary words you learned from *Oxford Word Skills*.
*   **Day 11: Saturday, June 13 (Speaking Part 2)**
    *   **Task 1 (45m):** Prepare notes for 3 different Speaking Part 2 topics (1 min prep). Record yourself speaking for 2 minutes for each.
    *   **Task 2 (60m):** Listen to an episode of **IELTS Energy English 7+** podcast.
    *   **KPI:** Speak for the full 2 minutes without repeating your ideas.
*   **Day 12: Sunday, June 14 (Rest & Reevaluate - 30 mins)**
    *   **Task:** Review Error Log. Identify your weakest reading question type (e.g., True/False/Not Given) to focus on next week.

### 🟡 Week 3: Increasing Intensity (June 15 - June 21)

*   **Day 13: Monday, June 15 (Targeted Reading)**
    *   **Task 1 (45m):** Do 1 Unit of *Oxford Word Skills*. 
    *   **Task 2 (60m):** Practice *only* your weakest Reading question type using *The Official Cambridge Guide*.
    *   **KPI:** Improve accuracy on your weakest question type by 20%.
*   **Day 14: Tuesday, June 16 (Full Listening Test)**
    *   **Task 1 (45m):** Listen to **BBC 6 Minute English**. Summarize the topic out loud in 1 minute.
    *   **Task 2 (60m):** Do a FULL Listening Test (4 sections) from *Cambridge IELTS 10*. Transcribe (write down) any sentences you completely missed.
    *   **KPI:** Score 25+ / 40 (Band 6.0 level).
*   **Day 15: Wednesday, June 17 (Writing Timed Practice)**
    *   **Task 1 (30m):** Review grammar mistakes from past essays in your Error Log.
    *   **Task 2 (75m):** Write 1 Task 1 AND 1 Task 2 back-to-back. Give yourself exactly 60 minutes.
    *   **KPI:** Complete both essays within the 60-minute time limit.
*   **Day 16: Thursday, June 18 (Full Reading Test)**
    *   **Task 1 (20m):** Review vocabulary notebook.
    *   **Task 2 (85m):** Do a FULL Reading test (3 passages) from *Cambridge IELTS 11*. Strict 60-minute timer. Spend 25 mins reviewing mistakes.
    *   **KPI:** Complete all 40 questions in 60 mins. Score 26+ / 40.
*   **Day 17: Friday, June 19 (Advanced Essay Writing)**
    *   **Task 1 (30m):** Watch an **IELTS Advantage** video on "Complex Sentences".
    *   **Task 2 (75m):** Write one full Task 2 essay. Consciously include 2 relative clauses (who/which/that) and 1 conditional sentence (If...).
    *   **KPI:** Successfully use 3 complex grammatical structures.
*   **Day 18: Saturday, June 20 (Speaking Part 3)**
    *   **Task 1 (45m):** Watch an **IELTS Liz** video on Speaking Part 3 strategies.
    *   **Task 2 (60m):** Practice Speaking Part 3 questions out loud. Use the strategy: Answer, Explain, Example.
    *   **KPI:** Provide examples for every answer you give.
*   **Day 19: Sunday, June 21 (Rest & Reevaluate - 30 mins)**
    *   **Task:** Review Error Log. Compare Week 2 and Week 3 mock test scores.

### 🟡 Week 4: The 30-Day Milestone (June 22 - July 2)

*   **Day 20: Monday, June 22 (Reading Speed Drill)**
    *   **Task 1 (30m):** Do 1 Unit of *Oxford Word Skills*.
    *   **Task 2 (75m):** Do 2 Reading Passages from *Cambridge IELTS 11*. Give yourself only 35 minutes instead of 40.
    *   **KPI:** Finish both passages in 35 mins. This trains your brain to work faster.
*   **Day 21: Tuesday, June 23 (Listening Deep Dive)**
    *   **Task 1 (45m):** Listen to **IELTS Energy English 7+** podcast.
    *   **Task 2 (60m):** Do Sections 3 & 4 of a Listening test from *Cambridge IELTS 11*. Play the audio at 1.25x speed if possible.
    *   **KPI:** Catch the answers even when the speakers are talking fast.
*   **Day 22: Wednesday, June 24 (Writing Review)**
    *   **Task 1 (45m):** Do 2 Units of *Grammar in Use* (Articles: a/an/the). 
    *   **Task 2 (60m):** Take your essay from Day 17. Rewrite it completely to fix all grammar mistakes and improve vocabulary.
    *   **KPI:** Produce a flawless, corrected version of an old essay.
*   **Day 23: Thursday, June 25 (Reading Full Test)**
    *   **Task (105m):** FULL Reading test (60 mins) from *Cambridge IELTS 12*. Spend 45 minutes doing deep Error Log analysis.
    *   **KPI:** Score 28+ / 40.
*   **Day 24: Friday, June 26 (Writing Task 2 Under Pressure)**
    *   **Task 1 (25m):** Watch a Band 7.5 sample essay breakdown on YouTube.
    *   **Task 2 (80m):** Write a Task 2 essay in exactly 35 minutes (forcing yourself to be faster). 
    *   **KPI:** Finish the essay with 5 minutes left for proofreading.
*   **Day 25: Saturday, June 27 (Full Mock Speaking)**
    *   **Task (105m):** Do 3 complete mock Speaking tests (Part 1, 2, and 3). Record all of them. Listen back and grade yourself.
    *   **KPI:** No hesitations longer than 2 seconds.
*   **Day 26: Sunday, June 28 (Rest & Reevaluate - 30 mins)**
    *   **Task:** Rest up. Big mock test days are coming.

*   **Day 27: Monday, June 29 (Milestone: FULL READING MOCK)**
    *   **Task (105m):** Complete Reading Test from *Cambridge IELTS 13* under strict exam conditions (no phone, 60 mins exactly). Review errors.
    *   **KPI:** Log your score. Target: 29+ (Band 6.5/7.0).
*   **Day 28: Tuesday, June 30 (Milestone: FULL LISTENING MOCK)**
    *   **Task (90m):** Complete Listening Test from *Cambridge IELTS 13* under strict exam conditions. Review errors.
    *   **KPI:** Log your score. Target: 30+ (Band 7.0).
*   **Day 29: Wednesday, July 1 (Milestone: FULL WRITING MOCK)**
    *   **Task (90m):** Write Task 1 and Task 2 back-to-back in 60 minutes. Use the official IELTS answer sheet if possible.
    *   **KPI:** Complete both on time with full word counts.
*   **Day 30: Thursday, July 2 (Month 1 Evaluation Day)**
    *   **Task (105m):** You have finished Month 1! Today, you do NO new tests. You will spend 2 hours reading your entire Error Log from Day 1 to Day 29. 
    *   **KPI:** Identify the top 3 mistakes you are still making, and create a plan to fix them in Month 2.
