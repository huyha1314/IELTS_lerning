# ✅ The Elite IELTS Daily Checklist

Use this checklist every single day you study. It combines your core study plan and your gamified vocabulary quests into a single sheet. 

## ☀️ Morning / Commute (Micro-Quest)
- [ ] **The Loot:** Read or listen to an English podcast/text for 10 minutes.
- [ ] **The Extraction:** Extract 3 new words and add them to Anki/Quizlet along with the sentence they were found in. 

## 💻 During Work / Lab (Micro-Quest)
- [ ] **The Sticky Bomb:** Pick 3 words from your vocabulary list. Put them on a sticky note on your monitor.
- [ ] **The Deployment:** Force those 3 words into an email, Slack message, or conversation before you leave the lab.

## 🎯 The Main Study Session (60-75 Minutes)
- [ ] **0:00 - 0:05 | The Setup:** Phone in another room. Books on desk. Timer ready.
- [ ] **0:05 - 0:20 | Deep Work Input (15m):** Grammar structure, vocabulary review, or strategy video (e.g., IELTS Liz/Advantage).
- [ ] **0:20 - 0:25 | The Brain Break (5m):** Stand up, look away from the screen, stretch.
- [ ] **0:25 - 0:70 | Deep Work Output (45m):** Timed practice test or writing task. Force yourself to use the new grammar/vocabulary from your input session.

## 🔬 The Autopsy (Crucial 10 Minutes)
- [ ] **Score:** Grade your practice test. 
- [ ] **Log Errors:** Write every wrong answer in the **Error Log**.
- [ ] **Analyze Why:** For every wrong answer, write *why* it was wrong (e.g., Vocabulary gap? Careless spelling? Ran out of time?).
- [ ] **Aggressive Spelling Check:** Did I misspell any words today? Add them to the spelling list.

## 🌙 Night Time (Micro-Quest)
- [ ] **The Nap-Time Hack:** Complete a 5-minute fast-paced Anki session right before closing your eyes to sleep. 
- [ ] **Set Up Tomorrow:** Put tomorrow's book (e.g., **[B1] Grammar** or **[T9] Test 9**) directly on your keyboard.

---

### 📊 End of Week KPI Scorecard (Sundays Only)
- [ ] **Completed 5 High-Intensity Sessions?** (Or took my Flex day wisely)
- [ ] **Zero Spelling Errors on Mock Test?**
- [ ] **Defeated the Boss?** (Used 5 new vocab words correctly in a Saturday Writing/Speaking test)
- [ ] **Updated the Strategy?** (Looked at the #1 weakness in the Error Log and decided what to focus on next week)
